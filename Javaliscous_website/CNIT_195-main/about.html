Welcome to Javaliscious!

Angela opened Javaliscious in 2013 not knowing what to expect. She only envisioned a small coffee shop serving a delicious cup of coffee with a warm toasty bagel to her customers in her beloved Castro community in San Francisco.

Javaliscious is a warm and friendly coffee shop to enjoy your coffee and pastry. It's spacious and airy to meet up with friends, work, or just sit outside and get vitamin D. We always strive to make your coffee with care and respect just the same way we treat our employees and customers.

Make sure to stop by and say hi! We hope to see you soon.
